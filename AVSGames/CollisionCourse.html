Download the app

Also available on Itch.io

Collision Course is the journey of a flying red cube.

Instructions

R to restart level

Before Launch:

Arrow Keys to aim/change camera angle

WASD to change the cube's rotation

Spacebar to Launch in direction camera is facing

Camera Controls (After Launch):

WASD or Arrow Keys to turn the camera

C to reset the camera direction

O & P to change the Camera Position:

  • Launch point
  • Following player
  • 1st person/Inside player

Level Composition

Obstacles

Obstacle

A gray rectangular prism

Bounce Block

A yellow cube that bounces the Player Cube off of it.

Black Hole

A black sphere that sucks the Player Cube towards it. If it touches it, you lose, and the level restarts.

Teleporter

A purple sphere that teleports the player to another purple sphere.

Goal

Reach the cyan square that the Player Cube to complete the level.
